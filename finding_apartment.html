Finding an appartment in Berlin

Finding an apartment in Berlin can be challenging. The competition for getting flat is high and the rents are more and more expensive each year. Still, compared to other european cities, rents in Berlin remain resonably affordable.

Where to look

Required paperwork

The landlord might want to ask you for following documents:

  • A copy of your passport or ID
  • Payslips for the previous 3 months
  • A copy of your employment contract
  • Mietschuldenfreiheitsbescheinigung (document that proves that you don’t have any debt with your previous landlord)
  • A SCHUFA credit report (document that proves that you don't have any unpaid debts)
